About

Passion-driven
design approach

Hi — I'm Roman. A designer and illustrator based in the Czech Republic, with a practice that spans UX/UI, branding, game assets, and illustration. I believe design is most powerful when it balances clarity with character.

My work is shaped by two passions: drumming, which gives my design a rhythmic sense of timing and flow, and gaming, which pushes me to build immersive, world-complete visual systems.

Experience

Professional
journey

2026
Flexographic Prepress Specialist
DMF s. r. o.

I focused on preparing graphic data for flexographic printing. Based on customer artwork, I prepared production-ready files using knowledge of overprint, trapping, and other technical requirements necessary to ensure accurate transfer to printing plates. The goal was to guarantee that the final plates could reproduce the design on various flexible materials (labels, bags, packaging films, and other soft substrates) with maximum precision. I also assisted in the production of printing plates and engraved cylinders.

Prepress · Print

UX/UI Designer

The goal was to unify multiple system modules, define a new visual direction, design a consistent icon set, and ensure coherence across complex workflow wizards used for dental procedures and clinical projects.

UX/UI · Product Design
